// 01 — About

What is Berkeley Build Guild?

The Berkeley Build Guild is a gathering of teen makers building cool hardware projects in a fast, friendly, creative sprint. Teams of 2–3 get a set of electronics supplies — including a programmable microcontroller — and 90 minutes to turn an idea into reality.

Whether you're a seasoned coder or have never touched a circuit board, you're welcome here. Come alone, bring a friend, or meet your team on the day. No experience required.

// 03 — Kit

Your Free Kit

Each team receives these components — yours to keep after the event.

🎛️

Raspberry Pi Pico W

Programmable microcontroller with Wi-Fi, headers pre-soldered. Supports MicroPython & C++.

1× per team
🔌

400-Pin Breadboard

Full-size solderless prototyping board. Connect components without soldering — perfect for quick iteration.

1× per team
🔊

Mini Speaker

Compact speaker module for generating sound, music, or alerts from your project.

1× per team
📺

Mini OLED Screen

Tiny display for showing text, graphics, or real-time data from your project's sensors.

1× per team
